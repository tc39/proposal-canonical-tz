Time Zone Canonicalization proposal

This proposal aims to:

  • Improve consistency across ECMAScript engines with regard to time zone identifiers, so that programs will behave more similarly across engines
  • Make ECMAScript programs more resilient to changes in time zone identifiers, like 2022's change of Europe/Kiev to Europe/Kyiv

This specification consists of two parts:

  • Amendments to ECMA-262, stacked on top of the Temporal proposal
  • Amendments to ECMA-402, also stacked on top of the Temporal proposal.

To provide feedback on this proposal, please visit the proposal repository at https://github.com/tc39/proposal-canonical-tz.
